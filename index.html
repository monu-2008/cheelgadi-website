Our Story

A vision born on 30 January 2005

Cheelgadi Veg Restaurant Pvt. Ltd. was founded by Mr. D.P. Meena and inaugurated by the Ex Education Minister of Rajasthan, Mr. Ghanshyam Tiwari.

Located on Airport Road, Jaipur, Cheelgadi is celebrated for exceptional pure vegetarian cuisine — and for the extraordinary experience of watching aircraft ascend and descend while you dine.

The chief attraction is its high standard of cuisine and captivating rural Rajasthani ambience that has earned global appreciation.

"As the customer steps in, he has a feeling of comfort — as if he is at his own home."
